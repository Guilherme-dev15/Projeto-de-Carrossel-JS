Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and aspires to become the Pirate King. He possesses the Gomu Gomu no Mi Devil Fruit, which grants him rubber-like abilities. Known for his boundless optimism, bravery, and unwavering determination, Luffy leads his crew on adventures across the Grand Line.

Marshall D. Teach

Marshall D. Teach, also known as Blackbeard, is a notorious pirate and one of the Four Emperors. He is the only person known to wield the power of two Devil Fruits: the Yami Yami no Mi, which allows him to control darkness, and the Gura Gura no Mi, which grants him the ability to create earthquakes. His ambition and ruthlessness make him a formidable adversary.

Sabo

Sabo is the Chief of Staff of the Revolutionary Army and the adopted brother of Monkey D. Luffy and Portgas D. Ace. After eating the Mera Mera no Mi, he gained the ability to control fire. Sabo is dedicated to overthrowing the corrupt World Government and protecting the oppressed.

Gol D. Roger

Gol D. Roger, known as the Pirate King, was the only person to reach the end of the Grand Line and discover the legendary treasure One Piece. His execution ignited the Great Pirate Era, inspiring countless pirates to search for his treasure. Roger was known for his incredible strength, charisma, and indomitable spirit.

Shanks

Shanks, also known as Red-Haired Shanks, is one of the Four Emperors and a former member of Gol D. Roger's crew. He is known for his powerful Haki and laid-back personality. Shanks played a pivotal role in inspiring Luffy to become a pirate and continues to influence events in the One Piece world from the shadows.

Roronoa Zoro
